Intelligent Multimedia Security Group

Intelligent Multimedia Security Group is attached to the Third Research Laboratory, Institute of Information Engineering，Chinese Academy of Sciences. Under the leadership of Dr. Shiming Ge, our research focuses on computer vision, deep learning and artificial intelligence security, especially machine learning and its practical application with insufficient information. Students interested in artificial intelligence, deep learning and computer vision are welcome to join us!

智能多媒体安全课题组隶属于中国科学院信息工程所第三研究室。本课题组在葛仕明博士的带领下，主要研究方向为计算机视觉、深度学习与人工智能安全， 尤其关注信息不充分条件下的机器学习问题及其现实应用。欢迎有志于从事人工智能、深度学习和计算机视觉的学生报考博士和硕士研究生。

Research Interests

  • Low quality visual analysis and understanding, including: masked face recognition, low resolution object analysis, visual tracking, etc
  • Image generation and forgery detection, including: generation countermeasure network, face/body generation, Deepfake detection, etc
  • Big data controlled Shared learning, including: small sample/weak (self) supervision/confrontation/fairness/federation/causal learning, etc
  • 低质量视觉分析理解，包括：蒙面人脸识别、低分辨率物体分析、视觉跟踪等
  • 图像生成与伪造检测，包括：生成对抗网络、人脸/人体生成、Deepfake检测等
  • 大数据受控共享学习，包括：小样本/弱(自)监督/对抗/公平/联邦/因果学习等

Deep Visual Tracking

Visual Tracking has a wide range of applications in intelligent security, traffic monitoring, behavior recognition, military and medical. Deep learning based object trackers need to achieve accurate localization and robust tracking under complex dynamic conditions such as illumination changes, severe occlusion, scale changes and motion blur.

Controllable Representation Learning

Many uncontrollable problems in real scenes seriously limits the application of the representation learning, such as the unexplainability for human, the vulnerability to attack, the difficulty for learning from imperfect data, and other scenarios where the effective, fair and safe representation is very hard to learn.
